You’re offline

This page appears when your browser can’t reach the Study Planner server. In normal use, Study Planner can work offline after your first successful visit because the app shell is cached by a service worker. If this is your first time here, reconnect and reload once so the app can be cached for offline use. If the dashboard still doesn’t load, reconnect and try again shortly.

Your study data is stored locally in your browser by default, so you won’t lose your subjects or tasks just because you’re offline. However, features that require the network (like account login, cloud sync, or sending a message through the contact form) will only work again after you reconnect.

If you already opened the dashboard on this device before, try going back to the cached pages directly. When the connection is back, the app will automatically load the latest version.

Offline checklist (quick things to try):

  • Open the dashboard again (it may be cached): go to “Back to dashboard”.
  • If you used Calendar or Stundenplan before, open those pages too; they can be available offline after a successful online visit.
  • Avoid clearing site data while offline; that would remove locally stored subjects and tasks.
  • When you’re back online, consider exporting a snapshot as a manual backup before switching devices.

Need help? Use the contact page when you’re online again. For transparency about cookies, analytics, and optional advertising, see the privacy policy and terms.
